# Data and imagery attribution

- Earth texture: Three.js `earth_atmos_2048.jpg`, based on NASA Earth imagery. NASA's Blue Marble true-color global imagery is freely available to the public. Credit: NASA Earth Observatory.
- City names and coordinates: GeoNames `cities15000` data, filtered locally to cities with at least 500,000 residents plus national capitals. Licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0. © GeoNames contributors.
- Country borders and coastlines: Natural Earth 1:110m Admin 0 countries. Natural Earth vector data is in the public domain.
